Open Deck Rules

This isn’t just a game, it’s an open invitation. Open Deck is your icebreaker, your conversation starter, your way in. So take a turn, take a breath, and take a chance.

Setup:

  • Gather your group (recommended 5 or more players).
  • Take turns drawing and following instructions found on the cards.

Gameplay:

  • The first player draws a card to start the game.
  • Players take turns reading and following the instructions.
  • Play continues either clockwise or counter clockwise.
